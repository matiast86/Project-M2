About me

Matías Tailler

Age: 38

Profession: English teacher with 15 years of experience

Hello! I'm Matías, a passionate English teacher with a solid 15-year career in education. Throughout my career, I have had the privilege of helping countless students achieve their language goals, which has given me profound professional satisfaction.

A while ago, I discovered my interest in programming and decided to embark on this new adventure. I completed courses in Java and Python, where I acquired fundamental skills in software development. Currently, I am enrolled in Module 2 of the Full Stack Development program at Soy Henry, an institution renowned for its rigorous program and practical approach to developer training.

My goal is to combine my teaching experience with my new programming skills to create innovative and efficient solutions. I am an enthusiastic learner, always willing to face new challenges and continuously improve.

In addition to my interest in technology, I enjoy movies and love analyzing films, which has influenced the creation of this website.
